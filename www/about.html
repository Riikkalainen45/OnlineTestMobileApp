Mindfulness is a new way of looking the world around you. Did you know that people who take only 5 minutes of their day to meditate. Are happier, healthier and more succesfull.

Breathe in through your nose and out through your mouth. Let your thoughts come and go.

Each time at your work or life you have a negative though, choose moment to stop, lable the thought unnecessary and release negativity.

Pay more attention what you are doing. Appreciate your efforts. Eaven the most boring tasks are easier for you if you pay attention to them.
